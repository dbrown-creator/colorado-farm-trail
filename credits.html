Where the data comes from

This map stands on the shoulders of a directory that real people rebuild every single season. Here's who to thank.

🙏 Thank you, Colorado Proud

Every farm, market, U-pick and roadside stand you see on Colorado Farm Trail comes from the Colorado Proud Farm Fresh Directory, published by the Colorado Department of Agriculture.

The directory isn't a one-time list — it's a genuinely robust, community-driven effort that happens every year. Growers submit and update their listings, and the Colorado Proud team gathers, verifies and maintains hundreds of farms, markets and stands across all ~36 counties, keeping it current as the season grows. That yearly legwork is the reason a map like this is even possible. We're grateful for it.

🗺️ How this map uses it

The Farm Fresh Directory is the single source for every stop shown here. We take that public data and lightly clean and categorize it — sorting listings into map-friendly types (farmers' markets, CSAs, U-picks, wineries and more) so the pins are color-coded and easy to filter.

We refresh from the directory periodically, since it's community-submitted and keeps growing through the season. New and updated listings there flow into this map over time.

🚜 Run a farm, market or stand?

The best way to appear on this map is to be listed in the official directory — that's where our data comes from. If you're a Colorado grower, get yourself on the Colorado Proud Farm Fresh Directory and you'll show up here on the next refresh.
