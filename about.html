🌽 Colorado Farm Trail

About this project

A free, independent map for finding local food across Colorado — who we are, and the story of the data behind it.

🌽 What this is

Colorado Farm Trail is a free, independent map that helps you find local farms, farmers' markets, U-picks, roadside stands and wineries across Colorado — all in one place, right on your phone.

This is our project — an independent community effort, built by folks who love Colorado's growers and wanted an easier way to find them. We're on a mission to connect people with the local food grown around them, and just as much, to build and maintain the most up-to-date database of Colorado growers there is. The two go hand in hand: the better the data, the easier the find — and the more of your grocery budget lands in the hands of the growers in your own community.

🗺️ The data — and how to be part of it

Colorado Farm Trail started from the Colorado Proud Farm Fresh Directory — the public list of farms and markets published by the Colorado Department of Agriculture. That gave the map its foundation: hundreds of Colorado farms, markets, U-picks and stands to build around on day one.

From there, we grow on our own. We independently collect, verify and maintain our own listings — correcting details, keeping hours and seasons current, and weaving in new sources as we go. Here's where things stand:

  • Live Colorado Proud Farm Fresh Directory — the foundation the map launched on.
  • Live Submissions from growers — listings added and updated directly through this site.
  • Planned Colorado Farmers Market Association (CFMA / MarketWurks) — next round.
  • Planned USDA local food directories — part of our expanded all-Colorado farmers' market dataset.

🙏 Thank you, Colorado Proud

Colorado Farm Trail wouldn't exist without the Colorado Proud Farm Fresh Directory, published by the Colorado Department of Agriculture. It gave this project its starting foundation — hundreds of Colorado farms, markets, U-picks and stands to build a map around on day one.

The directory isn't a one-time list — it's a genuinely robust, community-driven effort that happens every year. Growers submit and update their listings, and the Colorado Proud team gathers, verifies and maintains hundreds of listings across all ~36 counties, keeping it current as the season grows. That yearly legwork is the reason a project like this could launch at all. We're grateful for it.
